Account & Data Deletion Instructions

Fewer Days — Migraine & Symptom Tracker

1. In-App Immediate Data Deletion

You can permanently delete all your logged symptom records, migraine history, and app preferences directly inside the app at any time without needing to contact support:

  1. Open the Fewer Days app on your Android device.
  2. Tap on the Settings icon (bottom navigation bar or top header).
  3. Scroll down to the Data & Privacy section.
  4. Tap Delete All Data.
  5. Confirm the prompt. All your local symptom entries, notes, and preferences will be immediately and irreversibly purged from your device.
Note: Deleting your data in-app is permanent. Once wiped, your historical tracking records cannot be recovered.

2. Requesting Cloud / Account Data Deletion

If you used Google Account Sign-In for cloud synchronization, you may also request the complete deletion of your cloud database records and account account profile from our servers via email:

Email Address: hermeslekkasdev@gmail.com

Subject Line: Data Deletion Request - Fewer Days

Required Information: Please provide the email address associated with your Google Account used in Fewer Days.

Turnaround Time: All cloud backup records, sync tokens, and associated account profile data will be permanently wiped from Google Firebase servers within 48 hours of request receipt.

3. Data Retention Policy

Because Fewer Days is built on a local-first architecture:

  • When you delete your data inside the app, no copies remain on your device.
  • When an account deletion request is processed, all synced cloud records are wiped permanently. We do not keep retained backups or log archives of user health entries.
